## Design Context

### Users

**Tutor (R, B2B-1)** — Premium physics tutors preparing students for EGE/OGE exams. Hourly rate 3000-4000 RUB. Manage 15-50 students in mini-groups. Core frustration: 3+ hours/evening on homework checking, feedback writing, and logistics instead of teaching. Need AI as a draft+action tool, not a chat-only output. Context: tired after lessons, working evenings, switching between Telegram/WhatsApp/email.

**Student (S, B2C-1)** — 16-18 year old high schoolers on their final EGE physics sprint. Mobile-first (phone is primary device). Core frustration: stuck on problems at 11pm with no one to ask; ChatGPT gives direct answers that kill learning. Need Socratic hints, not solutions. Emotions: anxiety about exam, motivation fragile, need visible progress to keep going.

**Parent (P, B2C-1)** — Paying 48,000+ RUB/month for tutoring, comparing ROI vs online schools at 5,000 RUB/month. Core frustration: "black box" between lessons — no visibility into daily practice. Need objective proof that money works 24/7, not 3 hours/week. Want to delegate control to the system instead of becoming a "supervisor" that damages family relationships.

### Brand Personality

**Serious. Trustworthy. Sharp.**

Voice: confident expert assistant, not a chatbot or a toy. Closer to a senior colleague than a customer support agent. Never condescending, never overly cheerful. Speaks concisely, acts decisively.

Emotional goals:
- Tutor: relief ("the routine is handled"), control ("I see everything, AI works my way")
- Student: confidence ("I can do this"), momentum ("I'm making progress")
- Parent: calm ("the system works, my money is well spent")

### Aesthetic Direction

Visual tone: clean, professional, focused. A workspace for serious exam preparation — not a game, not generic SaaS.

References: Brilliant.org (streaks, visual learning), Khan Academy/Khanmigo (Socratic AI chat), Teachworks (tutor dashboard professionalism).

Anti-references: Duolingo (too playful), generic AI chat interfaces, purple gradients, emoji-heavy UI.

Theme: light mode only. Accent green (#1B6B4A) conveys growth and progress without being flashy.

Typography: Golos Text — Russian-designed, strong Cyrillic. No Inter, Roboto, Arial.

### Design Principles

1. **Action over output** — Every AI result must have a next action (approve, edit, send). No dead-end screens.
2. **Tutor stays in control** — AI generates drafts; tutor approves. Never bypass the human.
3. **Mobile-first for students** — Students live on phones. Every student-facing screen must work perfectly at 375px. Inputs 16px+ (iOS Safari zoom prevention).
4. **Calm density for tutors** — Tutors manage 30+ students. Dense information display with clear hierarchy, not excessive whitespace.
5. **Progress is visible** — Every interaction should contribute to a sense of forward momentum. Streaks, completion states, green checkmarks.
